What Drives Price Variation?

Cell Source

PRP ($500-$2,000) is most affordable. BMAC ($2,600-$7,000) is mid-range. Adipose-derived ($5,000-$15,000) and Umbilical Cord ($8,000-$25,000) are premium options.

+ $1,500 - $15,000

Image Guidance

Ultrasound guidance adds $500-$800. Fluoroscopy (X-ray) guidance adds $800-$1,200 for precision placement.

+ $500 - $1,200

Facility Type

Private office is base price. Ambulatory surgery centers add $1,000-$2,500. Hospital outpatient adds $2,000-$5,000 in facility fees.

+ $2,000 - $5,000

Pricing Methodology

Our data is normalized using a weighted average of three primary sources:

  • Direct Clinic Fee Sheets (40%)
  • Patient-Reported Receipts (35%)
  • Industry Surveys (25%)
