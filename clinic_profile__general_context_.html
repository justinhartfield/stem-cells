About the Clinical Approach

Pacific Coast Regenerative Orthopedics specializes in non-surgical orthopedic care for athletes and active adults. They utilize a multi-disciplinary approach combining image-guided injections with specialized rehabilitation protocols.

This clinic primarily utilizes Bone Marrow Aspirate Concentrate (BMAC) and Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP). They do not utilize umbilical cord or amniotic products, adhering to current FDA guidelines regarding birth-tissue products for orthopedic use.

Regulatory Note

"The clinic reports using autologous (patient's own) cells. FDA Section 351/361 regulations apply. Treatment is considered 'off-label' in many orthopedic contexts. Patients are advised to request the clinic's specific safety data and regulatory compliance documentation before scheduling."
