About the Clinic

Regenexx Los Angeles specializes in advanced, image-guided regenerative orthopedic procedures. As part of the national Regenexx network, they focus on high-dose stem cell and platelet treatments for joints, spine, and soft tissues.

Unlike "one-size-fits-all" clinics, this location performs comprehensive functional assessments to determine if you are a viable candidate. Their protocols include multi-step cell processing in an onsite lab rather than using automated desktop centrifuges.
