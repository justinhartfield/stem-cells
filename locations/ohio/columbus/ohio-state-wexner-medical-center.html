About This Clinic

Ohio State Wexner Medical Center is a academic regenerative medicine provider located in Columbus, Ohio. They offer stem cell therapy and regenerative medicine treatments for various orthopedic conditions including knee osteoarthritis, spine and disc issues, shoulder injuries, and hip problems.

Specialty

Academic Regenerative Medicine

Treatments Offered

  • PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) Therapy
  • Bone Marrow Aspirate Concentrate (BMAC)
  • Adipose-Derived Stem Cell Therapy
  • Regenerative Joint Injections

Conditions Treated

  • Knee Osteoarthritis
  • Spine & Disc Degeneration
  • Shoulder Injuries (Rotator Cuff)
  • Hip Osteoarthritis
  • Sports Injuries
