About This Clinic

Cedars-Sinai Kerlan-Jobe Institute is a sports medicine & orthobiologics provider located in Los Angeles, California. They offer stem cell therapy and regenerative medicine treatments for various orthopedic conditions including knee osteoarthritis, spine and disc issues, shoulder injuries, and hip problems.
