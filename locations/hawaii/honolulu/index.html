For residents of Honolulu, Hawaii—a city with a population of approximately 350,000—stem cell therapy represents a growing field within orthopedic medicine and sports injury treatment. Stem cell clinics in Honolulu primarily focus on regenerative medicine techniques designed to address conditions such as knee osteoarthritis, joint pain, and sports-related injuries. Due to the city’s island location, the availability of advanced stem cell therapies can be somewhat limited compared to mainland facilities.
